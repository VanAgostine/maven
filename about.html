Nhu Lam

Maven

I still vividly remember my first time traveling overseas at 15, when I visited Italy and Greece with my high school English class. Looking back, I realize we stayed in a rundown hostel on the outskirts of Rome, with its worn yellow marble steps, an odd little bidet with a handheld hose, and no air conditioning.

But what truly stands out isn't the accommodations — it's the thrill of discovery. My first bite of thin-crust Italian pizza, the melodic flow of the romance language, and the profound realization that the world was so much bigger and more fascinating than my small little corner in Colorado.

That adventure changed me forever, and I have been chasing that sense of uniqueness in every corner of this earth. Now I get to channel that passion into creating journeys — crafting vacations that are not just memorable, but that, hopefully, spark a little bit of that same life-changing magic.
