Return of the Travel Agent

We have a theory: the internet didn't replace travel advisors—it convinced everyone to do more work for less. Somewhere along the way, planning a vacation became a burden. We compare hotels, decode thousands of reviews, second-guess every decision, and call it saving money.

Except… it usually isn't.

In most cases, you'll pay the very same rate whether you book on your own or through a Maven. The difference is what happens beyond the reservation. We replace endless research with firsthand expertise, trusted relationships, and thoughtful recommendations tailored to you. Then we elevate your stay with exclusive amenities like room upgrades, complimentary breakfasts, resort credits, and the kind of service that doesn't end once you've clicked "Book."

Because the greatest luxury isn't finding the perfect trip.
